About Us

At 21-20, our mission is bold, but simple: create the comprehensive vaccine management solution.


1. Simplifying the Vaccination Process


Our platform makes it easy to schedule vaccination appointments using a centralized appointment management platform where you can check your eligibility and find nearby vaccine administrators, displaying appointment availability and vaccine inventory. After your appointment, you will be issued a QR code-based digital vaccination certificate to verify your shot and manage your record.

2. Reopening The World


The pandemic has had a devastating impact on the American life. Schools have closed, small businesses are struggling to stay afloat, and millions of Americans have lost their jobs.

21-20 will assist in re-instating our way of life in 2-ways. The first is by providing 21-20 users a digital, verified vaccination certificate that can be scanned and verified on their 21-20 profiles. The second is by aggregating and reporting vaccination data to the states so that government and health officials can make an informed decision as to when it is safe to resume our normal way of life.

3. Spread Awareness


The greatest threat to the vaccine rollout’s success is the willingness of people to take an approved vaccine. In December, a Pew Research Center reported that only 60% of Americans would definitely or probably get a COVID-19 vaccine. Those who opted out cited safety concerns and / or a lack of trust in the development of the vaccine.

We strive to use our platform to educate the public about the vaccine as well as provide a portal to report any adverse reactions.
